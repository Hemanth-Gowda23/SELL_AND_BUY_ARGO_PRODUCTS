# Sell and Buy Agro Products

> A web application designed to help farmers improve productivity and profitability by connecting buyers and sellers of agricultural products directly.

---

## Overview

Sell and Buy Agro Products is a platform that bridges the gap between farmers and buyers — removing middlemen and enabling direct agricultural transactions. It combines a marketplace interface with best-practice sharing, helping farmers make more informed decisions about pricing and selling.

**The problem it solves:** Farmers in India often lose significant margin to intermediaries. This platform gives them a direct channel to buyers and access to community knowledge.

---

## Features

- Product listing and browsing for agricultural goods
- Buyer and seller registration and profiles
- Transaction facilitation between farmers and buyers
- Best practice sharing — farmers can post and read tips
- Search and filter by product category, location, and price
